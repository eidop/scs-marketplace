# Monetization Strategy: Gratis Audit → Oppsett

**Date:** 2026-04-18
**Status:** Approved
**Target:** Norske SMB-er (1-10 ansatte)
**Horizon:** 3-6 months to meaningful revenue

---

## Strategy Overview

Offer free 15-minute automation audits to Norwegian SMBs. Show them exactly how many hours they lose to manual processes. Sell a custom n8n setup package (25-80k NOK).

Core advantage: Technical competence is the differentiator. The audit proves value before any money changes hands.

---

## Sales Funnel

1. **Identify** — Find Norwegian SMBs with visible manual processes (job listings, slow support, outdated websites)
2. **Contact** — Cold LinkedIn message or email: "Hei, jeg ser dere ansetter — har dere automatisert rekrutteringsflyten? Jeg tilbyr en gratis 15-min audit."
3. **Audit meeting** — 15 min via Google Meet. Map their biggest pain point, show one relevant n8n demo.
4. **Offer** — Send written proposal within 24 hours with price, timeline, expected ROI.
5. **Delivery** — Custom n8n workflow delivered in 1-2 weeks.
6. **Follow-up** — Check in 2 weeks post-delivery. Request testimonial.

---

## Audit Meeting Structure (15 min)

| Minute | Activity |
|--------|----------|
| 0-3 | Quick intro — "Not a sales pitch, just showing you what's possible" |
| 3-8 | One specific pain: "What takes the most time in your daily operations?" |
| 8-13 | Live demo of one relevant n8n template solving exactly that |
| 13-15 | ROI numbers + "I'll send you a proposal tomorrow" |

## 90-Day Execution Plan

### Month 1: Foundation
- Week 1-2: Build 3 demo-ready n8n workflows (lead capture, recruitment, support)
- Week 2-3: Create proposal template, ROI calculator spreadsheet, post-audit email sequence (3 emails)
- Week 3-4: Start outbound — contact 5 Norwegian SMBs per week via LinkedIn

### Month 2: First Sales
- Week 5-6: Run 4-6 audit meetings, send proposals within 24 hours
- Week 7-8: Deliver first setup package, collect testimonial

### Month 3: Scaling
- Week 9-10: Use testimonial in new outbound round (10 contacts/week)
- Week 11-12: Target: 2-3 closed deals, potentially 50-160k NOK

### Weekly Time Allocation
- 60% outbound (contacting businesses, following up)
- 20% audit meetings
- 20% delivery (building workflows for customers)

## Key Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Low response rate on cold outreach | Personalize every message with company-specific observation |
| Customer expects ongoing support for free | Clearly scope deliverables in proposal, offer paid support add-on |
| n8n self-hosting complexity for customer | Offer hosted n8n as add-on or recommend cloud version |
| Pricing feels high for small SMBs | Start with Starter package, show ROI to justify |
