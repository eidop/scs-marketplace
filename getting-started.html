üîß Step 1: Choose Your Template

Browse our template library and select an automation that fits your needs. For this guide, we'll use a simple email notification workflow.

  1. Go to our Template Store
  2. Filter by category (e.g., "Sales" or "Marketing")
  3. Click on a template to preview
  4. Click "Import to n8n"

üîó Step 2: Connect Your Accounts

After importing, n8n will prompt you to connect your accounts:

  • Click on each node in your workflow
  • Select "Create New Credential" or choose an existing one
  • Follow the OAuth/API key flow for each service
  • Test each connection with the "Test" button

‚öôÔ∏è Step 3: Customize Your Workflow

Tailor the template to your specific needs:

  • Variables: Map data fields between services
