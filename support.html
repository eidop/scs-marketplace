Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect Memo-Bot to my Slack workspace?
Navigate to the Integrations tab in your dashboard, select Slack, and follow the OAuth flow. Detailed steps can be found in our Setup Guide.
What LLM models does SCS support?
We currently support GPT-4o, Claude 3.5 Sonnet, and Gemini 1.5 Pro. You can switch models in the Config section of your dashboard.
Is my data used for training public models?
No. SCS uses Enterprise API endpoints with strict zero-retention policies. Your business data remains yours.
